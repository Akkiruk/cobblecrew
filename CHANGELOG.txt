## 3.0.13

## 3.0.11

- Pokémon like Dugtrio and Sandslash (which lack "physical") now correctly fall back to "cry" or other available animations

## 3.0.10

- Idle Pokémon at their origin now play ground_idle; after 30 seconds of boredom they switch to unamused/sleep
- Defenders play an angry reaction when spotting a hostile
- Deposit arrival, success, and work-complete moments each trigger distinct reactions
- Debuff-style defense jobs (Repeller, Fearmonger, Sentry, Poison/Ice Trap) use special/status animations instead of physical
