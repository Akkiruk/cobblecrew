# CobbleCrew Job Tier List & Balance Audit

> Rated by: **How much a real player would actually want/use this job**
> Factors: Item value, demand frequency, automation savings, uniqueness, setup effort

---

## S Tier — Busted Good, Everyone Wants These

| Job | Category | What It Does | Why It's S Tier |
|-----|----------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Ore Miner** | Gathering | Mines ore blocks (coal, iron, gold, diamond, etc.) | Free ores forever. Diamond pickaxe loot. Infinite passive mining is the #1 thing every player wants. |
| **Vein Miner** (combo) | Gathering | Breaks up to 16 connected ore blocks at once | Ore Miner but on steroids. 16 blocks per action is absurd throughput. |
| **Fortune Miner** (combo) | Gathering | Mines ores with Fortune III | Fortune III on diamonds/lapis/redstone automatically forever. Combined with the ore miner, this is an infinite diamond factory. |
| **Ore Smelter** | Processing | Raw iron/gold/copper → ingots | Pair with Ore Miner = infinite ingots. Saves all your furnace fuel forever. |
| **Blast Furnace** (combo) | Processing | Raw ores → ingots at **2x rate** | Double ingot output is insane. 1 raw iron = 2 iron ingots? That's duplication. |
| **Crop Harvester** | Gathering | Auto-harvests mature wheat/beetroot/etc. and replants | The single most universally wanted farm automation. Everyone grows crops. |
| **Growth Accelerator** | Environmental | Random-ticks crops 3x per visit | Massively speeds up all farms in range. Stacks with crop harvester for a fully automated fast farm. |
| **Bonemeal Applicator** | Placement | Applies bone meal to crops/saplings from containers | Another crop speedup. Takes bone meal from chests and uses it automatically. |
| **Berry Harvester** | Gathering | Auto-harvests Cobblemon berries | Berries are the #1 Cobblemon consumable. Every competitive player needs berry farms. |
| **Fishing Looter** | Production | Generates fishing loot table drops (10% treasure) | Passive treasure fishing. Enchanted books, name tags, saddles — all AFK. |

### S Tier Balance Suggestions

- **Ore Miner / Vein Miner / Fortune Miner**: These three together can strip-mine a region. Consider:
  - Add a **daily ore cap** per Pokémon (e.g., 64 ore blocks/day) so it doesn't replace all mining
  - Fortune Miner should have a **longer cooldown between harvests** (maybe 2x normal gathering speed)
  - Vein Miner's 16-block limit is fine, but add a **cooldown after each vein** (30-60 seconds) so it can't chain veins instantly
- **Blast Furnace (2x output)**: This is literally item duplication. Nerf to **1x output but 2x speed** (processes twice as fast, same yield) or give it a 25% chance to produce a bonus ingot instead of guaranteed 2x
- **Growth Accelerator + Bonemeal Applicator + Crop Harvester stacking**: When all three work the same farm it's absurd. Consider making Growth Accelerator apply only **1 random tick** instead of 3, or add a per-block cooldown so the same crop can't be accelerated repeatedly
- **Fishing Looter**: 10% treasure chance every 120s is fine, but the treasure table includes enchanted books and rare items. Consider lowering treasure chance to **5%** or increasing cooldown to **180s**

## A Tier — Very Useful, High Demand

| Job | Category | What It Does | Why It's A Tier |
|-----|----------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Overworld Logger** | Gathering | Chops logs (top-down) | Wood is always needed. Top-down harvest means full trees get cleared properly. |

## Balance Recommendations

### Nerfs for S Tier (Make Less Dominant)

1. **Blast Furnace 2x output → 1x output, 2x speed**
   - Currently a literal dupe. Change `input.count * 2` to `input.count` but halve the processing cooldown. Still better than normal smelting, but not free resources.

2. **Ore Miner / Fortune Miner / Vein Miner — add diminishing returns**
   - After mining X blocks in a session (configurable, default 32), the Pokémon gets "exhausted" and won't mine again for 10 minutes. Prevents infinite strip-mining.
   - Fortune Miner should have 1.5x the normal gathering cooldown between targets.

3. **Growth Accelerator — reduce random ticks from 3 to 1**
   - 3 random ticks is too much. 1 tick per visit is still a significant boost without being a crop-acceleration machine.

4. **Fishing Looter — increase cooldown when treasure drops**
   - Normal loot: 120s cooldown (unchanged)
   - Treasure loot: 300s cooldown (2.5x penalty after getting treasure)

### Buffs for F Tier (Make Worth Using)

1. **Scale Shedder → add dragon scale or rare material**
   - Replace prismarine shard with a chance for a Cobblemon-relevant item (evolution stone fragment?)
   - Or: 10% chance to drop a nautilus shell alongside the scute

2. **Gem Crafter → increase emerald output**
   - 2 emeralds + 2 amethyst shards instead of 1+2. Or add a 10% chance for diamond.

3. **Milk Producer → produce milk bottles (stackable) instead of buckets**
   - If milk bottles aren't vanilla, just give 4 cookies instead (Miltank-themed food). Or make it produce cheese (a custom food item worth 8 hunger).
   - Simple fix: change output to `Items.CAKE` every 300s. Everyone likes free cake.

4. **Chorus Fruit Harvester → also produce popped chorus fruit**
   - Auto-smelt 50% of harvested chorus fruit into popped chorus fruit (used for purpur blocks and end rods). Gives the job actual building material value.

5. **Glowberry Picker → increase yield to 3-5 berries per harvest**
   - 1 glow berry is worthless, but a stack of them is decent for cave decoration and light.

6. **Cactus Pruner → also produce green dye alongside cactus**
   - Bonus 1-2 green dye per harvest (auto-smelting the cactus). Makes it a dye factory.

7. **Mushroom Forager → chance to produce mushroom stew or suspicious stew**
   - 20% chance to produce mushroom stew instead of raw mushrooms. Adds food value.

8. **Demolisher (combo) → add bonus cobblestone/stone drops**
   - Make it drop 2-3x the normal stone drops to differentiate it from Stone Breaker. It requires 2 moves, it should feel impactful.

9. **Gem Vein Finder (combo) → increase diamond chance to 10%, add unique drops**
   - 5% diamond is too low for a combo job. Bump to 10% and add prismarine crystals or lapis blocks as possible drops.

10. **Silk Touch Extractor (combo) → expand target categories**
    - Let it work on ORE blocks too (getting ore blocks instead of raw materials). Silk-touched diamond ore, spawner blocks, etc. would be genuinely valuable.

### General Observations

- **Too many production jobs that generate cheap items.** Feather Molter, Spore Releaser, Fruit Bearer, Egg Layer — these produce items that are trivially farmable in vanilla. Consider buffing quantities (5 feathers, 3 apples, 4 eggs) or adding rare bonus drops.
- **Defense jobs are largely pointless** because mobs near a pasture aren't a real threat. Guard is fine, everything else is outclassed. Consider letting defense Pokémon have an **XP-generating** component — mobs killed by guards drop XP orbs that get stored in a container.
- **Support buffs are great for party Pokémon but mediocre for pasture.** You're at your base — you don't need combat buffs. Consider adding **work-boosting auras** where support Pokémon near a pasture boost the speed of other workers in the same pasture.
- **Combo jobs that produce decorative items feel like wasted combo slots.** If a Pokémon has 2 specific moves, the reward should be significant. Candle Maker, Chain Forger, Lantern Builder, and Banner Creator should either produce more valuable items or produce them in larger quantities.
