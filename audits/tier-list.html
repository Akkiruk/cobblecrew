CobbleCrew Job Tier List

130 jobs rated by real player value — item value, demand frequency, automation savings, uniqueness, setup effort

⬇️ Nerf Suggestions (S Tier)

Blast Furnace 2x → 1x output, 2x speed

Currently literal duplication. Change to same yield but double processing speed. Still better than normal smelting without being free resources.

Ore/Fortune/Vein Miner — diminishing returns

After mining 32 blocks in a session, Pokémon gets "exhausted" for 10 min. Fortune Miner should have 1.5x gathering cooldown between targets.

Growth Accelerator — 3 ticks → 1 tick

3 random ticks per visit is too much. 1 tick is still a significant boost without being a crop-acceleration machine.

Fishing Looter — treasure penalty cooldown

Normal loot: 120s. Treasure loot: 300s (2.5x penalty after getting treasure).

⬆️ Buff Suggestions (F Tier)

Scale Shedder

Add evolution stone fragment or 10% nautilus shell chance alongside scute.

Gem Crafter → 2 emeralds + 10% diamond

Double emerald output or add diamond chance to justify the job slot.

Milk Producer → cake every 300s

Milk buckets don't stack & clear buffs. Change to producing cake instead.

Chorus Fruit → auto-smelt 50% to popped

Popped chorus fruit for purpur blocks gives it building material value.

Glowberry → 3-5 per harvest

1 is worthless, a stack is decent for cave decoration and light.

Cactus Pruner → bonus green dye

1-2 green dye per harvest (auto-smelting). Becomes a dye factory.

Mushroom Forager → 20% stew chance

Chance to produce mushroom stew adds food value.

Demolisher → 2-3x stone drops

Requires 2 moves so it should feel impactful. More drops differentiates from Stone Breaker.

Gem Vein Finder → 10% diamond + unique drops

5% is too low for a combo. Add prismarine crystals or lapis blocks.

Silk Touch Extractor → ORE blocks

Let it mine silk-touched ore blocks. Diamond ore etc. would be genuinely valuable.

General Observations

  • Too many cheap production jobs. Feather Molter, Spore Releaser, Fruit Bearer, Egg Layer produce trivially farmable items. Buff quantities (5 feathers, 3 apples, 4 eggs) or add rare bonus drops.
  • Defense jobs are largely pointless — mobs near a pasture aren't a real threat. Consider letting guards generate XP orbs stored in containers.
  • Support buffs are great for party, mediocre for pasture. You're at base — you don't need combat buffs. Consider work-boosting auras that speed up nearby workers.
  • Combo jobs producing decorative items waste combo slots. Candle Maker, Chain Forger, Lantern Builder, Banner Creator should produce more or produce something valuable.
